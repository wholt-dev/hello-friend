# Claim the Faucet

The LitDEX faucet drops **0.01 zkLTC + 10 points** every 24 hours. It is the easiest way to get gas for testing.

> This is a testnet faucet. zkLTC has no monetary value.

## Eligibility

To prevent bot abuse, a claim requires **both** of the following:

- ✅ Hold at least one LitDEX NFT (`LitShard`, `LitCore`, or `LitGod`)
- ✅ Own at least one `.lit` domain on the LiteForge registry

If either is missing, the claim button shows **"NFT + .lit Domain Required"** and the modal lists which check failed. The on-chain transaction never even attempts to fire — eligibility is enforced by the backend before sending.

## How to claim

1. Click the **Faucet** button in the top nav.
2. Make sure both eligibility ticks are green.
3. Press **Claim 0.01 zkLTC + 10 Points**.
4. The success card shows the explorer link for the transaction.

The cooldown timer counts down from 24 hours. Try again after that.

## How do I get the prerequisites?

| Need | How |
| --- | --- |
| LitDEX NFT | Buy with points from the [NFTs page](/nfts/). LitShard costs 1,000 pts and is the easiest tier to reach. |
| `.lit` domain | Register on the [Hub Buy page](/hub/lit-domain). 1 year is 0.05 zkLTC. |

If you are completely new and have zero zkLTC, ping the team in [Telegram](https://t.me/litdex_discussion) — manual top-ups are available for new wallets.

## Why the gating?

Earlier the faucet only required a 24-hour cooldown. Bots farmed it heavily. The NFT + `.lit` requirement raises the bar enough that only real users qualify, while keeping the cost low (a single LitShard mint + a 1-year domain).
