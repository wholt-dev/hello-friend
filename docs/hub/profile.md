# Profile

The profile page is the public-facing card for any `.lit` user. From your own profile you can manage names, set a bio, and review transfer history.

## Sections

- **Identity card** — avatar (auto-generated from name), bio, owned names, primary name selector
- **Transfer history** — every zkLTC transfer to/from this wallet, scoped per wallet so swapping accounts shows the new wallet's history only
- **Activity feed** — recent posts, recent messages, recent market actions (read directly from chain)

## Set bio

1. Click **Edit profile** on your own profile.
2. Type a short bio (max 200 chars).
3. Sign. The bio is stored in the registry and visible on your profile to anyone who visits.

## Set primary name

If you own multiple `.lit` names, pick one as primary:

1. Open your profile.
2. Click **Set as primary** next to the name.
3. Sign. The wallet now reverse-resolves to that name everywhere.

## View someone else's profile

- Click any `.lit` name in the global feed, market, or messenger.
- The profile page shows their bio, owned names (if public), and recent activity.
- An **Add friend** button is at the top right if you aren't friends yet.

## Privacy

Bios are public — visible to anyone. Owned names are public. Transfer history is public on chain (anyone with your wallet address can see it on the explorer), but the profile only shows transfers that involve `.lit` names or known dApp interactions to keep the feed readable.

> The profile page is also the entry point for marketplace actions on your names — there's a "List for sale" button next to each owned name.
